Use of Pokédex

Encyclopedia

Displays various information about Pokemon species, including elemental types, typical sizes and locations they can be found in the wild.

Tracker

Keeps track of the Pokemon you've seen and caught. Pokémon you’ve caught appear in color. Pokémon you’ve seen but haven’t caught appear as silhouettes.

Stats

Shows the pokemon's stats including Name, Type of pokemon, Weight, Ability, Hit Points, Attack, Defense and Speed.
